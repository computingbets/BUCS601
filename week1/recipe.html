Broccoli Bacon Quiche

recipe link - allrecipes.com

John Staley's French Egg Nachos recipe

ingredients

  • pie crust
  • 4 eggs
  • 2 cups of milk
  • 2 cups broccoli
  • bacon to taste, cooked
  • salt
  • pepper
  • cheddar to taste
  1. preheat oven to 350F
  2. beat eggs well
  3. add milk
  4. mix egg and milk
  5. pour milk and egg mixture into pie crust
  6. add broccoli
  7. add cooked bacon
  8. add cheddar
  9. cook in over on cookie sheet for 30-60 min, till firm
